About the job
Job Description

We’re looking for a Lead Platform Engineer to take ownership of our Kubernetes-based multi-cluster internal developer platform that powers mission-critical microservices across the organization.

This role is equal parts technical leadership, hands-on engineering, product thinking, and communication. You will shape the direction of the platform as a product, obsess over developer experience, and ensure the platform is intuitive, reliable, and well-documented.

You will work closely with product teams, architects, and engineering managers to evolve our platform into a highly automated, self-service ecosystem that accelerates developer productivity and enables secure, seamless deployments at scale.

Our technical stack includes Azure Kubernetes Service, Envoy Gateway, Opentelemetry Operator, LGTM Stack, Crossplane, KEDA, Cert Manager, External DNS, Kyverno, External Secrets Operator, Argo CD, Helm, Bicep, Github Actions.



Qualifications

WHAT YOU'LL DO

Own the technical direction of our Kubernetes platform with a strong focus on developer experience and operational excellence. 
Treat the platform as a product. This involves gathering feedback, identifying friction, and continuously improving workflows and interfaces. 
Design, build, and operate cloud-native infrastructure with an everything-as-code mindset. 
Write production-quality code (Bash, YAML, Bicep, Github Actions) - this is a hands-on engineering role. 
Drive complex features and refactoring efforts across provisioning, deployments, observability, and cluster lifecycle management. 
Partner closely with product teams to understand real workflows and deliver a frictionless self-service experience. 
Mentor engineers through collaboration and example, fostering a culture of curiosity, ownership, and learning. 
Raise the bar on maintainability and engineering craftsmanship through strong attention to detail.

5+ experience in platform engineering, SRE, or application engineering roles. 
Deep expertise in Kubernetes, containers, and the cloud-native ecosystem. 
A builder mindset - you write and ship code regularly. 
Excellent technical writing skills. 
Experience operating in public cloud environments (Azure, AWS, or GCP) with modern IaC and GitOps tooling. 
Proven track record improving developer workflows, self-service capabilities, or internal platforms. 
Understanding of networking (TLS, DNS, HTTP, GRPC, proxies). 
Experience with modern observability practices and tooling (instrumentation, metrics, logs, tracing, profiling). 
Pragmatic, collaborative, and humble approach to technical leadership. 
High attention to detail and a bias for architectural simplicity and clarity.

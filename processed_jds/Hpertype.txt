About The RoleAs a Platform engineer at Hypertype you’ll design, operate, and evolve the cloud foundation that keeps our AI products fast, reliable, and cost-efficient. You’ll own GCP architecture (networking, security, deploys), Terraform-based IaC, observability, and SLOs. Your days will involve shaping golden paths for services, improving CI/CD, and partnering with engineers to keep latency and unit costs down as we scale.

What You’ll Do

Build and maintain secure, scalable infrastructure on GCP (Kubernetes, VPC, Pub/Sub, etc.).
Define and manage Terraform modules, CI/CD pipelines, secrets and safe rollouts/rollbacks.
Establish observability (logs, metrics, tracing), SLOs/SLIs and on-call playbooks; drive incident response improvements.
Optimise performance and cost (autoscaling, casing, connection pooling, query tuning, storage cycle).
Collaborate with Software and ML Engineers to set targets, debug production issues and improve developer experience.

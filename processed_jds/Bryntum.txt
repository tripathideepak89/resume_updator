About the job
Bryntum’s components are used in applications at thousands of companies worldwide, for scheduling, planning and operations. Those systems depend on infrastructure that is reliable, secure and easy to maintain.

That's where you come in.

Bryntum's JavaScript components are embedded in business-critical systems at 5,000 companies across 80 countries. Keeping that infrastructure reliable, secure and scalable is the foundation everything else rests on.

Bryntum is looking for a Senior DevOps Engineer who takes that responsibility and wants to help us build for the next phase of growth.

About the role
You'll join a small, sharp DevOps team and take on shared responsibility for the infrastructure that keeps Bryntum running. You'll have access to production servers and the systems our customers depend on. We need someone who is thorough, dependable, and takes pride in doing things properly.

We're in the middle of a maturity journey: moving towards AWS, consolidating infrastructure and replacing ad hoc solutions with more robust setups. You'll be part of shaping that transition alongside your colleagues, with real ownership over the decisions you make.

Today we run a mix of physical servers in data centers in the Netherlands and the US alongside virtual infrastructure. You'll work across both, with a focus on consolidation, reliability and keeping things simple to maintain.

What we're looking for
5+ years of experience in DevOps or infrastructure engineering
Experience with GitHub
Solid experience with AWS, Docker, and cloud infrastructure
Comfortable with scripting and automation, Node.js and Bash in particular
Experience managing both physical and virtual server environments
A methodical, detail-oriented approach

Meritorious
What matters is that you're not afraid of new technology and can get up to speed quickly. Experience using AI tools like Claude Code for scripting, debugging or operational workflows is a big plus. We’re interested in people who actively use modern tools to move faster and reduce manual work.

Fluent English is a requirement, Swedish is a plus.

Who you are
You're driven, take pride in your work and set a high bar for yourself. You enjoy working independently and act proactively even under uncertainty. You're the kind of person who spots a problem and fixes it. You find it genuinely satisfying to keep systems clean, stable, and well-organized.

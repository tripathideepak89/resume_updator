What You'll Do

We are seeking a Senior Platform Engineer with strong hands-on experience in modern cloud-native software development across the CNCF ecosystem. This role involves building and evolving a complex, production-grade product that acts as a centralized control plane for all infrastructure components.

The ideal candidate will have deep Kubernetes and Go expertise, hands-on experience with production-grade GitOps workflows using Argo CD, and strong operational knowledge of running large-scale, enterprise infrastructure. You will work closely with platform, infrastructure, and developer experience teams to design and operate scalable, secure, and highly observable systems in complex production environments.

Experience in platform engineering or developer experience (DevEx) teams is highly preferred.

What You'll Bring

Strong hands-on experience operating Kubernetes in production, with a deep understanding of Kubernetes APIs, controllers, and extensibility. Extensive experience with Crossplane, including composition design and development as well as building custom Crossplane functions for infrastructure abstraction. Strong Go (Golang) development skills, with hands-on experience building backend services, controllers, APIs, and custom Kubernetes operators. Proven expertise in GitOps practices using production-grade Argo CD, including designing and maintaining GitOps-based deployment and infrastructure workflows. Solid understanding of Backstage, its system model, and experience integrating it with internal platform tooling. Hands-on experience with observability tooling, including strong working knowledge of Grafana Cloud for metrics, logs, and traces. Extensive experience designing and operating workloads on Microsoft Azure, along with strong operational knowledge of maintaining large-scale, enterprise-grade infrastructure, ensuring reliability, scalability, security, and operational excellence in production systems. Proven experience contributing to complex, production-grade products and building platforms that act as control planes for infrastructure components. Additional good-to-have experience includes working with AWS and OpenShift, building internal developer platforms and developer experience (DevEx) tooling, and knowledge of secrets management.

About the job
Senior Cloud DevOps Engineer (AWS)

About QoplaQopla was founded by restaurant owners in 2018 with a simple goal: to help restaurants run their operations more smoothly and intelligently. Today, more than 2,000 restaurants rely on Qopla for table ordering, self-service kiosks, and online ordering.

We build and operate business-critical systems used daily in real restaurant environments — where downtime directly impacts revenue. Reliability, stability, and scalability are at the core of everything we do.

The RoleWe are looking for a Senior Cloud DevOps Engineer (AWS) to take ownership of our existing cloud platform and infrastructure.

You will take over and evolve our AWS-based Kubernetes environment, ensuring it is extremely stable, highly available, and scalable — with a strong focus on zero downtime and proactive operations.

This is not a “set up from scratch” role — it’s about improving, hardening, and scaling a system already running in production at real scale.

This is primarily an on-site role based in our Solna office, working closely with the CTO. Some hybrid flexibility is possible, but we believe close collaboration is key for this role.

What You’ll Work WithOur platform includes:

Kubernetes (EKS) on AWS
Java (Spring Boot) services
Node.js / NestJS services
MongoDB, Redis
Kafka and event-driven systems
React and React Native applications

What You’ll Do

Take ownership of our AWS and Kubernetes infrastructure
Ensure high stability, uptime, and zero-downtime deployments
Proactively identify weaknesses and eliminate potential failures before they happen
Set up and improve monitoring, alerting, and observability
Design and maintain reliable backup and disaster recovery strategies
Optimize performance, scalability, and cost in production environments
Improve CI/CD and deployment workflows for safe and predictable releases
Work closely with developers to ensure systems are built for reliability from day one

What We’re Looking For

Strong experience running production systems on AWS and Kubernetes
Proven track record of building stable, highly available systems
Experience with microservices architectures and distributed systems
Solid understanding of observability, monitoring, and incident response
Experience with databases like MongoDB and Redis in production
Experience working with event-driven systems (e.g., Kafka)
Pragmatic mindset — you focus on what actually works in production

Meriting:

Experience with on-premise infrastructure or hybrid cloud setups (we are exploring hosting some services ourselves)
Interest in hardware and infrastructure beyond the cloud
Willingness to take ownership of internal hardware (e.g., preparing and managing staff laptops and basic office infrastructure) as a smaller side responsibility since we still are a small company.

Why Qopla?

Real-world impact — your work runs in restaurants every day
Full ownership of a live, business-critical platform
Small, experienced team with fast decision-making
Focus on building long-term, stable systems — not hype

If you care about stability, uptime, and building systems that just work — every time — we’d love to hear from you.
